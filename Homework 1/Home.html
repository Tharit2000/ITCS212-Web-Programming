Welcome to My Dream Journey

There are a lot of places that I want to visit once in my life.

Akihabara, Japan

    As I am an anime lover, this is the place that I want to visit the most. In more recent years, Akihabara has gained recognition as the center of Japan's otaku culture, and many shops and establishments devoted to anime and manga are now dispersed among the electronic stores in the district.

Great Wall of China, China

    The Great Wall of China is an ancient wall in China. It is the longest structure humans have ever built. One day I want to walk on this wall, watching the beautiful views of mountain in the morning.

Pont des Arts, France

    The Pont des Arts or Passerelle des Arts is a pedestrian bridge in Paris which crosses the River Seine. Someday in the future, after I get married, I want to go honeymoon with my wife at the bridge of love.

Universal Studios, Singapore

    Universal Studios Singapore is a theme park that I had visited once with my classmate in high school. There were a lot of good memories. If possible, I would like to visit this place and having fun with my friends again.
